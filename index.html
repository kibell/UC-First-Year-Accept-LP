Accepted Student Days

Accepted Student Day (ASD) is a campus event designed for students who have been accepted to SNHU. Whether you’ve decided SNHU is the college for you or you’re still making your decision, ASD is a great way to learn more about our campus firsthand.

At ASD, you'll be able to:

  • Experience an academic class
  • Tour our residence halls
  • Participate in campus life activities
  • Explore our student support services
  • Meet other accepted students

Campus Tours

Let us show you around SNHU! You’ll get to chat one-on-one with an admission counselor, attend a brief information session and take a one-hour walking tour of campus, which includes stops at our library, dining hall, classrooms, residence hall, campus bookstore and athletics facilities.

Campus tours are Monday – Friday at 10 am and 2 pm, as well as on select weekend dates and evenings.
